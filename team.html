Meet Our Team

Michael Krzeminski - Managing Partner

For the last 7 years Michael has advised and represent both amateur and professionals in many facets of their careers.

On the professional side, Michael directs his efforts in providing a complete player management service. That includes player affiliation & marketing, negotiating endorsement contracts, as well as offseason training.

To amateur players, his focus is to act as a conduit of information and provide advice and guidance to the player and their family on all current and future matters of their career.

Michael resides in Chandler, Arizona

Steve Pierce

Certified by the Major League Baseball Player’s Association as a Player Agent for over three decades.

During his tenure, Steve negotiated over a hundred MLB player contracts, represented players in arbitration hearings while maintaining long term relationships with scouts, MLB front office executives and player development departments across the league. 

His love for the game of baseball, his laughter, smile, and positive attitude, will be missed by all who knew him.
